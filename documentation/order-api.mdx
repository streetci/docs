Our Delivery Order API is designed for e-commerce platforms that wish to outsource the delivery through our delivery services. We provide a comprehensive set of services to automate the creation, modification, retrieval, and deletion of delivery orders.

## Purpose

The primary purpose of the Delivery Order API is to support e-commerce platforms in managing their delivery orders seamlessly through our services. By integrating our API, e-commerce platforms can focus on their core activites.
