---
title: Documentation
description: Detailed descriptions of the different status codes used for tracking parcels in our delivery system.
---

## PENDING
The parcel is awaiting further action.
This status indicates that the parcel has been pre-recorded in our system and is awaiting additional information to validate the order

## REGISTERED
The parcel has been registered in the system.
This status indicates that the parcel has been successfully recorded in the system.

## CANCELED
The parcel delivery has been canceled.
This status indicates that the delivery of the parcel has been called off.

## ASSIGNED_FOR_COLLECTION
The parcel has been assigned for collection.
This status indicates that the parcel has been assigned to a delivery agent for collection.

## COLLECTION_IN_PROGRESS
The collection of the parcel is in progress.
This status indicates that the delivery agent is currently in the process of collecting the parcel.

## COLLECTED
The parcel has been collected successfully.
This status indicates that the parcel has been successfully collected from the sender.

## NOT_COLLECTED
The parcel could not be collected for various reasons (e.g., sender absent, incorrect collection information, etc.).
This status indicates that the collection attempt was unsuccessful.

## COLLECTION_POSTPONED
The collection of the parcel has been postponed.
This status indicates that the collection of the parcel has been rescheduled.

## WAREHOUSED
The parcel is stored in the warehouse.
This status indicates that the parcel is currently being stored in our warehouse.

## ASSIGNED_FOR_DELIVERY
The parcel has been assigned for delivery.
This status indicates that the parcel has been assigned to a delivery agent for delivery to the recipient.

## WAITING_FOR_DELIVERY
The parcel is waiting to be delivered.
This status indicates that the parcel is ready and waiting to be delivered.

## DELIVERY_IN_PROGRESS
The delivery of the parcel is in progress.
This status indicates that the parcel is currently being delivered to the recipient.

## DELIVERED
The parcel has been delivered successfully.
This status indicates that the parcel has been successfully delivered to the recipient.

## NOT_DELIVERED
The parcel could not be delivered.
This status indicates that the delivery attempt was unsuccessful.

## DELIVERY_POSTPONED
The delivery of the parcel has been postponed.
This status indicates that the delivery of the parcel has been rescheduled.

## REFUSED
The parcel was refused by the recipient.
This status indicates that the recipient refused to accept the parcel.

## ARTICLE_TO_RETURN
The parcel is marked for return.
This status indicates that the parcel is marked to be returned to the sender.

## ASSIGNED_FOR_RETURN
The parcel has been assigned for return.
This status indicates that the parcel has been assigned to a delivery agent for return.

## WAITING_FOR_RETURN
The parcel is waiting to be returned.
This status indicates that the parcel is ready and waiting to be returned.

## RETURN_IN_PROGRESS
The return of the parcel is in progress.
This status indicates that the parcel is currently being returned to the sender.

## RETURNED
The parcel has been returned successfully.
This status indicates that the parcel has been successfully returned to the sender.

## NOT_RETURNED
The parcel could not be returned.
This status indicates that the return attempt was unsuccessful.

## RETURN_POSTPONED
The return of the parcel has been postponed.
This status indicates that the return of the parcel has been rescheduled.

## LOST
The parcel is lost.
This status indicates that the parcel is lost and cannot be delivered or returned.

## DAMAGED
The parcel is damaged.
This status indicates that the parcel has been damaged during the delivery process, making it unfit for delivery or return.
